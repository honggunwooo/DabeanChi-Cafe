# DabeanChi ☕  
**Discover Coffee That Fits Your Taste**

DabeanChi is a coffee marketplace where users can **buy and sell coffee beans**, and more importantly, let **AI** recommend beans based on their personal taste.

Coffee can be overwhelming — origin, roast level, processing methods, flavor notes… it’s easy to get lost.  
We simplify the process so you can understand **what kind of coffee you actually like** and find it effortlessly.

---

## Features
- **Buy coffee beans** from various sellers and roasters  
- **List your own beans** for sale  
- **Receive AI-based recommendations** tailored to your flavor and aroma preferences  

---

## How the AI Works (Taste → Match)

### 1. Identify Your Preferences
You can express the flavors and aromas you enjoy, such as:

**Aroma Examples:**  
- fruity  
- floral  
- chocolatey  
- nutty  
- citrus  
- caramel  
- spice  

**Taste Elements:**  
- acidity  
- sweetness  
- bitterness  
- body  
- aftertaste  

### 2. Match With Available Beans
The AI then **searches through the beans currently available in the marketplace**  
and recommends the ones that best align with your taste.

> **In short:** You tell us what aromas and flavors you like — we find the right beans from what’s currently on sale.

---

## Why This Matters
If you’re going to drink coffee every day, it should be **something that truly fits your taste** — not just whatever is closest or most common.  
DabeanChi helps you explore confidently and understand your own flavor profile.
